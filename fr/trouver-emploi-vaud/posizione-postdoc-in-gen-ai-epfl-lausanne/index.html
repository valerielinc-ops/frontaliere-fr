Vue d’ensemble du poste

The Laboratory of Visual Intelligence for Transportation ( VITA ) is looking for a postdoctoral researcher in the area of Generative AI.

VITA research interests lie at the intersection of Computer Vision, Machine Learning (Deep Learning), and Human-Robot Interaction to redefine how machines interpret human actions, anticipate intentions, and interact seamlessly with people in various settings.

The successful candidate will be developing new methods for Real-time Video Generation, digital double, human-like avatars. Profile PhD in Computer Vision Background in CS

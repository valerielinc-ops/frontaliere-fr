Vue d’ensemble du poste

Mission For the 1 PhD position starting in 2026 summer (flexible) , we are seeking a candidate with experience in iontronic materials .

Examples include stimuli-responsive soft materials, biomaterials, hydrogels, organogels, organohydrogels, conductive polymers, ionic elastomers, and ionic liquids, among others.

The candidate should be interested in emerging, interdisciplinary fields and be motivated to address new challenges, including the development of (1) iontronic devices , (2) soft material manufacturing , and (3) biointerfaces .

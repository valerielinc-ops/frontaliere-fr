Processus de candidature

  • Mission The research engineer will be working with multiple teams at a technical level, predominantly developing and maintaining software frameworks for the control of robotic manipulators and machine learning applications.
  • The candidate will have the opportunity to design and implement solutions to the challenges posed by various students and research collaborators in the lab and actively participate in robotic experiments.
  • The role includes some opportunities for hardware work related to sensor interfacing and experimental design, based on the needs of the researchers.
  • Finally, the candidate will help champion and promote excellent software practices including code reviews and active use of version control across the lab.

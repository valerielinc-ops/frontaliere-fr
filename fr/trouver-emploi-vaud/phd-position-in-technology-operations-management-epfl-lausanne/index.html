Mission The Chair of Technology and Operations Management (TOM) at the College of Management is offering a full-time PhD student position within the EPFL doctoral school program.

Our research targets problems that arise in product assortment optimization, omni-channel inventory management, inventory theory, and demand forecasting.

We also address challenges in supply chain resilience and sustainability modeling, and supply network design.

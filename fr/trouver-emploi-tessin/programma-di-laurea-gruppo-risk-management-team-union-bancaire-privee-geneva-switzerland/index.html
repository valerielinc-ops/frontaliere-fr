Description originale

The Group’s risk management activities are overseen by Group Risk Management, led by the Group Head of Risk Management (Chief Risk Officer / CRO). In Switzerland, Group Risk Management teams are responsible for overseeing the Bank’s activities, while ensuring local alignment with Group standards at an international level. They coordinate methodologies, implement transversal risk management initiatives and lead key local projects in close collaboration with Group-level risk representatives. The Swiss Risk Management function is structured into specialized teams covering Treasury & Trading, Asset Management, Wealth Management and Operational Risk. In addition a dedicated Project team provides cross-functional support for key initiatives led by the CRO or team heads. Acting as a second line of defense, the Risk teams serve as trusted advisors to the Business, participate to local committees, perform independent controls and support the definition and implementation of mitigation plans in coordination with risk owners (1st line of defense).

Nous recherchons un stagiaire motivé en Acquisition de Talents pour rejoindre notre équipe pour un stage de 6 mois à Lugano ou Genève.

Exigences principales

  • Master’s degree (or currently enrolled in a Master’s program) in Human Resources, Business Administration, Psychology or a related field.
  • Previous experience (internship, part time role or similar) in recruiting, HR or employer branding is a plus.
  • Experience in data management, reporting or working with HR/ATS systems is an advantage.
  • Excellent command of English (spoken and written).
  • Good command of Italian or French is considered a strong plus, depending on the location.
  • Interest in working with HR tools and platforms (e.g. Applicant Tracking Systems, LinkedIn Recruiter, career websites).
  • Good MS Office skills, especially in Excel and PowerPoint, with an interest in reporting and basic analytics.
  • High attention to detail and accuracy in handling data and administrative tasks.
  • Strong interest in Talent Acquisition, the banking industry and investment management.
  • Excellent communication and interpersonal skills, with a natural focus on service and candidate experience.
  • Organised, structured and able to manage multiple tasks and priorities.
  • Proactive, curious and willing to take ownership of tasks and suggest improvements.

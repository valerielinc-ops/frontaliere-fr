Description originale

Your Purpose The Head of Learning & Development (Onboard & Ashore) and Talent Management (Ashore) is responsible for defining and leading the global strategy for Learning and Development across both shipboard and shore-based employees, while overseeing Talent Management and Succession Planning for shore-based populations only. The role ensures the company develops the capabilities required to deliver exceptional guest experiences, operational excellence, and strong leadership across a global workforce, including thousands of shipboard crew and a diverse shore-based organization. For shipboard employees, the role focuses on building world-class operational and service capabilities through structured training, leadership development, and career progression frameworks that support fleet performance and guest satisfaction. For shore-based employees, the role leads both Learning & Development and Talent Management, including leadership development, succession planning, and talent pipeline development for corporate and operational leadership roles. Working closely with senior operational leaders across Operations, Guest Experience, and Corporate Functions, this role drives a culture of continuous learning, leadership excellence, and organizational capability building across the company’s global operations. Your Impact Global Learning & Development Strategy (Onboard & Ashore) - Define and implement the global Learning & Development strategy covering both shipboard crew and shore-based employees. - Develop an integrated learning ecosystem that supports operational excellence onboard and leadership capability ashore. - Ensure learning initiatives directly support strategic priorities such as: - guest experience excellence - safety and compliance - operational performance - leadership effectiveness - digital and business transformation - Oversee onboarding and development frameworks to accelerate employee integration and performance across both shipboard and shore environment

Logistics Officer Swatch Group Logistics Officer About The Swatch Group

The Swatch Group is an international group active in the design, manufacture and sale of finished watches, jewellery, watch movements and components.

It is the world’s largest watchmaking group and supplies nearly all the components required for the watches sold by its 16 individual brands, including Breguet, Blancpain, OMEGA, Rado, Longines, Tissot, Mido, Swatch and Flik Flak.
